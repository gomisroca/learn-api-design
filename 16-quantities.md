# Quantities

- Quantities with clear units of measurement (bytes, km, etc.) **must** include the unit as suffix.
  - The unit **should** be abbreviated.
  - The abbreviation **should not** be plural.
- If the quantity is a number of items, the field **should** use the suffix `_count`. (e.g. `book_count`)

### Compound Units

- Quantities with compound units of measurement **may** use underscores to separate the units.
- Unabbreviated units **must** be separated.
- Abbreviated units **should not** be separated.
- Compound units **should** be in plural form.

### Inverse Units

- Inverse units **should** be indicated as compound units.
- The inverse unit **should** be in singular form.
