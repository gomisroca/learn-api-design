# Stability Levels

### Alpha

- An alpha component undergoes rapid iteration and changes.
- Alpha users **must** be toletant to change.
- The number of users **should** be curated.
- Breaking changes **must** be allowed.
- Users **must** have no expectation of stability.

### Beta

- A beta component **must** be considered complete and ready to be declared stable.
- They **should** be exposed to an unknown and potentially large audience.
- They **should not** be behind an allowlist. They **should** be available to the public.
- They **must** be permitted to change. These changes **should** be minimal.
- Backwards-incompatible changes **must** be made only after a reasonable period of time.
- Beta components **should** be time-boxed.

### Stable

- A stable component **must** be fully-supported.
- There **must** be no breaking changes.
